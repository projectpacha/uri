Collecting Words

Building a dictionary is more than just compiling words—it involves systematically organizing them based on meaning and usage. Traditional dictionaries list words alphabetically, but a more effective approach for language documentation is grouping them into semantic domains—categories based on shared meanings, such as nature, emotions, or tools. This method helps capture the relationships between words, making the dictionary more intuitive and insightful.
